Let's go Mario!

Here are 3 pro tips for playing Mario Odyssey.

  1. Tip 1: Fight enemies with a hat spin by shaking your controller

    2. You can do this whenever you want, but especially when you need to clear out the space around you. For example, maybe you need to knock out goombas around you.

  2. Tip 2: Find where you need to ground pound by following any dog with a hat.

    3. There aren't too many, but when you find one, it might lead you to a location where you can get power moons.

  3. Tip 3: Ground pound

    4. Do a ground pound by jumping with A or B and then pressing zl or zr. This can help you knock out goombas or collect power moons that the dog with a hat leads you to.
