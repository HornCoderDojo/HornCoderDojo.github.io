Mario Brothers Wii Game Review

The good and the bad.

The Good

The good thing about this game is that you can play up to 4 players and gets all fun and crazy and there are lots of worlds to choose from.

The Bad

The bad thing about this game is that after you finish the 8th world, there is a 9th world that is pretty hard.

Final Verdict

All in all, this is a pretty good game. I strongly recomend this game.
