Save the kingdom!

Here are 3 pro tips for playing Zelda.

  1. Tip 1:Defeat a camp of Bokoblins without getting to close

    2. For this tip to work you will have to find a small camp of Bokoblins when it's raining, drop a metal weapon such as a sword on the ground, be separated by a small mound from the Bokoblins camp, and use magnesis to make the sword go right on top of the camp of Bokoblins, and a lightning bolt might strike it and electrocute the camp.

  2. Tip 2: Get arrows and other stuff by breaking boxes.

    3. This tip can be used if you find any type of box, but sometimes there is nothing in the boxes.
